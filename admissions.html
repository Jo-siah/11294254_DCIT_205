Admissions

Welcome!

We are thrilled that you have expressed interest in the Computer Science Department's various degree programs. Our department invites excellent students from all backgrounds, including those from historically underrepresented groups in engineering, to consider University of Ghana for their graduate studies. In making admissions decisions, the CS Department will comply with the requirements of the law as determined by the Supreme Court of Ghana, evaluating each applicant based on their "experiences as an individual—not on the basis of race.” We continue to value a diverse student body that benefits the educational experience of our students and our mission of generating knowledge at University of Ghana.
